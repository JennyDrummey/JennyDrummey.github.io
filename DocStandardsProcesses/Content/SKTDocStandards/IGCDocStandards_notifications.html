SKT Notifications and Messaging

There are two types of notifications in SKT:

  • Email messages

  • In-app messages

Write email and in-app messages in the active voice, with the subject of the sentence performing the action. There is one exception:

“You have been assigned to [this asset] by [this person].”

In the example above, we want the user to know immediately that something has happened that affects them personally. It helps draw attention to the message when it begins with “you.”

Workflow - Review

PM Reviews Documentation

PM may review documenation on the Jira ticket, if it is a release note or a small update. For new features and significant updates, TWs create a google doc for PM to review.

When the content is ready for review, update the Documentation Status field to Review, and @ mentions PM on the ticket.

PM confirms that new and updated features are described completely and correctly from a user’s perspective.

Either:

  • PM approves the content by setting the Documentation Status field to Approved.

Or

  • PM notes on the Jira ticket what needs to be updated, and the process begins again at the In Process step.

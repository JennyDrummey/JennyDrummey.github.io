Send Spark Content

Internal Communication publishes a weekly, company-wide newsletter. Product has a spot in the newsletter that we use to highlight things we want the company to know about, such as:

  • New features

  • New versions

  • KB articles

  • Support statistics

  • New or interesting online help

NOTE: A link to the SKP release notes is included by default every week.

To submit Product content for Spark:

  1. Gather content for the week. Reach out to Support or the Doc team for ideas. Each item needs to contain a link for more information.

  2. Submit the content with links to InternalCommunications@syniti.com by Tuesday at noon.
